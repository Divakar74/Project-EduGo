Why EduGo?

EduGo is an online personalized learning/tutoring tool that enables students and tutors to communicate with each others for a better future.It is enabled with video call facility and Student Corner to develop as a community around the nation/world.It includes Tutors across the nation to connect with students for a better personalized learning tool.
